FAQ

Good questions we hear a lot

Working with us
Do I need to be tech-savvy to work with you?
Not at all. Most of our clients are experts in their own field — restaurants, retail, healthcare, construction — not in technology. We translate between the two so you never have to pretend to understand something you don't.
What kinds of businesses do you work with?
Small and mid-sized businesses across WNC — healthcare practices, breweries, retail shops, contractors, creative agencies, and more. If you have data you're not using or tasks you're doing by hand, we can probably help.
What does the free AI-Readiness Audit actually involve?
It's a 1-hour conversation where we walk through how your business currently runs — what's taking too long, what's unclear, and where data or AI tools could help most. You'll leave with a clear picture of your options, whether you hire us or not. No tech knowledge required, no sales pressure.
Costs & timeline
How much does this typically cost?
Projects start around $500 for a focused AI setup or workflow fix. Ongoing monthly support starts at $500/month. Everything is quoted clearly upfront — no surprise invoices.
How long does it take to see results?
Focused projects like an AI chat assistant or automated report typically take 1–3 weeks from kickoff to delivery. More complex builds like a full business intelligence dashboard may take 4–6 weeks. We always give you a clear timeline before we start.
Data & security
Is my business data safe?
Yes. We work within enterprise-grade tools like Anthropic's Claude for Teams, which comes with strict data privacy protections. We'll walk you through exactly what goes where before we touch anything.
Do you need access to all our systems?
Only what's needed for the specific project. We scope access carefully and document everything we connect to. You stay in control of your data at every step.
